Attribute Scope Type
avalanche Public
Notes: The detector detects avalanches, which may endanger the railway traffic.<br/>
cranks Public
Notes: The detector detects the presence of cranks for switch actuators at their normal storage location, e.g. in a special cabinet at the stationmaster.<br/>
derailment Public
Notes: The detector detects any derailed railway vehicle. It is often used in rear of tunnels or bridges to reduce the damages by derailed vehicles.<br/>
doors Public
Notes: The detector monitors the closed position of entry doors of equipment rooms<br/>
fire Public
Notes: The detector detects fire or smoke in equipment cabinets or rooms.<br/>
flatWheel Public
Notes: The detector detects any flat wheel of a railway vehicle<br/>
gas Public
Notes: The detector detects the excessive concentration of a particular gas in the vicinity<br/>
hotWheelBox Public
Notes: The detector detects any hot axle box of railway vehicles.<br/>
intrusion Public
Notes: The detector monitors the access doors to any equipment cabinet or room in order to detect unauthorised access.<br/>
landSlide Public
Notes: The detector detects landslides or rockfalls, which may endanger the railway traffic.<br/>
loadingGauge Public
Notes: The detector detects any railway vehicle exceeding the loading gauge due to replaced goods or similar.<br/>
weighing Public
Notes: The detector checks the axle load of any railway vehicle against a pre-set limit.<br/>
