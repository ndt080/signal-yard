Container for assets used for operation of interlockings and controllers. They represent a more functional/logical view onto the railway network but depending on the hardware as defined in infrastructure domain.<br/>All assets in one container (asset list) are owned by a single infrastructure manager within the railway network rather than by individual interlocking systems.<br/>
