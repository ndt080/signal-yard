Signals are used for traffic control. They transmit the commands from the control system to the train driver. In conventional systems they are physical objects at the track controlled by the interlocking having the possibility to show different optical aspects whose meaning is defined in the IMs operational rules.<br/>Depending on their position in the network and the use within routes the signals have a specific function in controlling the train traffic.<br/>With a signal there are several speed values connected which are completely independent of the particular aspect the signal is showing but solely depending on the infrastructure surrounding the signal.<br/>
