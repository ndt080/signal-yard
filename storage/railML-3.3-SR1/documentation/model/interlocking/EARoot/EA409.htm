The tunnel gate is a movable structure for completely closing the tunnel tube at the portal to cover the tunnel from bad weather conditions like icing or it intersects a tunnel to prevent ingress of water or smoke. It thus prevents train traffic in blockedClosed position. Dependent on train approach the gate is moved to passableOpen position. The gate movements and positions are controlled by the interlocking system in similar manner like a level crossing.<br/>
