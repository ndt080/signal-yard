The Extent of Control is one of full control, steering only, notification only or none.<br/>Full control implies that the IL controls the element AND reads the element feedback to check the execution of the control. <br/>Notification only means that the IL only reads the status of the object in terms of switch position left, controlled or boolean 1 or derailer engaged.<br/>Steering only means that the IL emits the control signal without checking for feedback.<br/>Note that LoC=none suggests that the IL has no relation at all with the track asset, indicating a modelling error that merits further investigation and/or explanation.<br/>
