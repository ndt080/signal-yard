organizations or companies (like infrastructure managers, railway undertakings, vehicle keepers, freight forwarders, ...) taking part in the railway business or operation. Each element may be used several times for several entries, as e.g. a network may be divided into areas with different infrastructure managers, and as within the network there will usually move vehicles from different producers.<br/>
